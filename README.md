## what this is

Deiadorebel is the musical expression of the Deiadora ecosystem — the moment the wave function is observed and everything researched collapses into sound. Each transmission is precise in the quantum sense: a signal that carries the structure of the system that generated it, tunnelling through whatever barriers classical mechanics says it cannot cross.

Every poem in the Field Notess series was always also a song. The compositions have existed for years — in notation, in memory, in the body — waiting for the technology to catch up with the vision. It has. The transmission begins now.

## the node

**Node 6 · transmission · dodecahedron** — quantum tunnelling. A particle passes through a barrier it has no classical right to cross — not over it, not around it, but *through* it — because its wave function extends beyond the barrier. Value transmitted between coherent fields without loss operates by the same principle.

The dodecahedron is the geometry of transmission at the largest scale — twelve pentagonal faces, the most complex of the Platonic solids, the one that most closely approaches the sphere. Its geometry is based on the golden ratio — the same mathematics that describes quasicrystalline tiling, the same proportion that appears in the spiral of a nautilus shell, the branching of a river delta, the arrangement of seeds in a sunflower.

The signal does not force its way through. It extends far enough that the barrier yields.

## design system

### accent colour
`#0f6e56` — deep water · algae · 500nm on the visible spectrum — blue-green, the colour of deep still water, the particular teal of something that has been there a long time and is not going anywhere.

### accent-lt
`#1a9e7a` — for hover states.

### CSS

- **Exo 2** — display, headings, kickers, labels
